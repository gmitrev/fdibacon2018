About Us

We are united through the hell that is the Technical University of Sofia. We were brave enough to study there and even survived Werkstoffkunde!

What is Our Goal?

Get together and drink beers. Remember the past through rose-tinted glasses and talk about Spilko. And maybe discuss technology and stuff.

  • Learn from the best FDIBA Alumni members
  • Have dedicated 1-to-1 meetings with the experts
  • Drink an unsafe amount of beer in attempts to forget the pain
  • Get a cool T-Shirt with the cool logo
